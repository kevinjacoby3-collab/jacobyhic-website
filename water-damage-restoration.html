What We Do

Rebuilding After the Water Is Gone

After your water remediation company removes the water and mold, your space still needs to be rebuilt. That's where we come in. We specialize in the renovation side — restoring your basement, bathroom, or any water-damaged area back to its original condition or better. We work alongside or after your water mitigation company to make your home whole again.

1

Framing & Structural Repair

Replace damaged studs, framing, and structural supports to restore the integrity of your walls and ceilings.

2

Drywall Installation & Finishing

New drywall install, taping, mudding, and seamless finishing so you'd never know there was damage.

3

Trim, Baseboards & Molding

Baseboards, crown molding, door casings, and window trim — all replaced and finished to match your home.

4

Flooring Replacement

LVP, tile, hardwood, or carpet to replace water-damaged flooring with materials built to last.

5

Door & Cabinet Replacement

Replace warped doors, swollen cabinets, and damaged vanities with quality replacements.

6

Painting & Final Finishes

Full interior painting, fixture installation, outlet covers, and all final detail work to complete the restoration.
